My Blog

My Journey into Programming

June 15, 2023

Starting my programming journey was both exciting and overwhelming. Coming from a small town in Nepal, resources were limited, but my curiosity was boundless...

I remember the first time I wrote a simple HTML page. It was just a heading and a paragraph, but seeing it render in the browser felt like magic. That moment sparked something in me.

As I progressed, I discovered CSS and JavaScript, and suddenly my static pages came to life. The more I learned, the more I realized how much there was to learn. This paradox was both humbling and motivating.

Transitioning to languages like C and Python opened new ways of thinking about problems. The structured approach of C contrasted beautifully with Python's flexibility, showing me that programming languages are tools, each with its own strengths.

What keeps me going is the joy of solving problems and creating things that didn't exist before. Every bug fixed, every feature implemented, is a small victory in this endless journey of learning.
